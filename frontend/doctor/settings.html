Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new patient?

Go to the Patients section from the bottom navigation. Tap the + Add Patient button and fill in the patient's details. You can link existing app users by searching their phone or email.

How do I start a video consultation?

Go to the Consult section and click Start New Consultation. A video call room will open. Share the link with your patient to let them join.

How do I view patient messages?

Tap Messages in the bottom navigation. All patient conversations appear here. Recent messages also show in the notification bell 🔔 in the top bar.

How do I export my data as a PDF?

Go to Settings → Data & Storage → Export Data. Click Export — an official Cogno Solution branded report opens in a new window. In the print dialog, choose Save as PDF.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Change Password. Click the Change button, enter your current and new password, and confirm.

How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Two-Factor Auth and toggle it on. Once enabled, you will receive an OTP to your email on every login.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Use the Dark Mode toggle in Settings → Appearance, or use the moon icon toggle in the profile dropdown in the top bar.

How do I manage appointments and schedule?

Open the Schedule section. You can view upcoming appointments, mark them complete, reschedule, or cancel by tapping any appointment card.

How do I generate medical reports?

Go to the Reports section. Click New Report to generate a report for a patient. Reports can be filtered by type and exported as PDF.

Who do I contact for billing or account issues?

Email support@cognosolution.com with your account email and a description. Our team responds within 24 business hours.
