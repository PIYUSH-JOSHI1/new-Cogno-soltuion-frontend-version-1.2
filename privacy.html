Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we handle your data with care.

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cogno Solution. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered learning platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a personalized learning experience:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and role (student, parent, or doctor).
  • Child Data: Date of birth and learning progress to tailor educational content.
  • Interaction Data: Games played, scores, and time spent on activities.
  • Communication: Messages exchanged between parents and specialists on our platform.

3. COPPA Compliance

As a platform serving children, we strictly adhere to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We require parental consent for users under the age of 13 and provide parents with tools to review or delete their child's data at any time.

4. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Personalizing AI-driven learning paths.
  • Providing progress reports to parents and authorized specialists.
  • Improving our gamified modules and exercises.
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing unauthorized access.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including end-to-end encryption for messaging and secure cloud storage provided by Supabase. We do not sell user data to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Parents can manage their child's profile through the Parent Dashboard or by contacting our support team.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@cognosolution.com.
